TASK 5
PREVENTION CHECKLIST


Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist:


- Always verify the authenticity of social media accounts before trusting their content
- Look for verified badges or cross-check accounts on official websites
- Do not blindly follow advice from influencers without fact-checking
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links shared by unknown or unverified accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts
- Use strong and unique passwords for different platforms
- Never share OTPs, banking details, or personal information online
- Regularly monitor your bank transactions and UPI activity
- Do not approve unknown UPI payment requests or collect requests
- Report suspicious accounts or cybercrime at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
