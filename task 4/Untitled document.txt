TASK 4
PART B: SOCIAL MEDIA CHECKLIST


DO’s:
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Keep profiles professional
- Verify information before sharing
- Respect others online
- Adjust privacy settings regularly


DON’Ts:
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information
- Don’t post inappropriate content
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying
- Don’t click on suspicious links
- Don’t overshare your location or daily routine
